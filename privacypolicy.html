Cookies policy 

HTTP cookies make web interactions easier. Cookies are small files of information on a website that is saved in the visitor's browser for subsequent retrieval by the website. It allows a server to determine whether visitors have returned to a website and enables the website to display customized settings and tailored content. We use cookies to analyze site activity and enhance our website content and services to make your experience on our website more personalized.

Most browsers include a simple process that allows you to decline or accept the cookie function if you do not want any data collected through the usage of cookies. However, if you do not set up your browser, you will accept cookies to this website.
